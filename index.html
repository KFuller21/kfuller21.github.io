Passing
5:30–5:45 · 15 min
Passing Triangles
Groups of 3. One player at each cone (~10 yards apart). Pass and move to an empty cone.
  • Stand at a cone. Player 1 passes to Player 2.
  • After passing, Player 1 runs to the empty cone.
  • Player 2 now passes to Player 3, then runs to the empty cone.
  • First 7 min: two-touch (control, then pass). Last 8 min: one-touch (pass right away).
💡 Key ThingAfter you pass — RUN. Don't watch the ball. Move to the empty cone immediately.

Set Pieces
5:45–6:00 · 15 min
Corner Kicks + Free Kicks
Walk through each set piece slowly first, then practice at full speed.
  • Corner kick: Kicker crosses the ball. A runs to near post, B stays at penalty spot, C drifts to far post. Run it 4 times each side.
  • Free kick: 3 defenders stand shoulder-to-shoulder as a wall, 10 yards from the ball. Kicker shoots over or around. 5 kicks per player.
  • Throw-in: Feet stay on the ground, throw from behind your head with both hands. Aim at your teammate's feet. Practice 3 each near the sideline.

Full Scrimmage — Game Shape
Split into 2 teams of 8. Rotate players in every 10 min so everyone gets time. Light contact — this is a rehearsal, not a match.

Warmup
5:30–5:42 · 12 min
Rondo — 4v2 Keep-Away
4 players on the outside try to keep the ball from 2 defenders in the middle. 1 or 2 touch only. If a defender steals it — the player who lost the ball goes in the middle.
  • Set up: 4 cones in a square (~10 yards each side). 4 players on corners, 2 in the middle.
  • Outer players: pass quickly — 1 or 2 touches. No dribbling.
  • After passing, move to a new spot on the square edge — don't stand still.
  • Defenders: work together as a pair — one presses, one covers.

Combination
5:42–5:57 · 15 min
Wall Pass (1-2 Combo)
Player A passes to B, then sprints forward. B returns the ball into space for A to run onto. Like bouncing off a wall.
  • A has the ball. B stands 10 yards away at an angle.
  • A passes to B (Pass ①), then immediately SPRINTS forward.
  • B returns the ball (Pass ②) into the space A is running into.
  • A receives and dribbles to the cone. Swap roles and go again.
💡 Key ThingA must sprint the MOMENT they pass. If they watch — it's too late. "Pass and GO!"

Crossing
5:57–6:17 · 20 min
Winger Cross → Strike
  • Winger starts at the touchline with the ball. 3 strikers wait at the edge of the penalty box.
  • When the winger reaches the orange cone (5 yards from end line) — strikers START their runs: A near post, B penalty spot, C far post.
  • Winger crosses LOW and HARD across the goal area.
  • Each group of 3 gets 5 crosses from the left side, then 5 from the right. Rotate every 10 crosses.
💡 TimingDon't run too early — you'll run past the ball. Wait for the cone, then go.

1v1 Defense
5:30–5:50 · 20 min
Force Outside — 1v1
Defender must stop the attacker from reaching the goal. Key rule: DON'T dive in early. Be patient and force them toward the sideline.
  • Cones 12 yards apart, 20 yards long. Attacker at one end with ball, defender 5 yards in front.
  • Defender runs toward the attacker, then SLOWS DOWN at 2 yards away. No diving yet.
  • Body position: low stance, knees bent, one foot slightly forward. Watch the ball — not the player's body.
  • Only tackle when the attacker touches the ball too far forward. Otherwise — delay and stay in front.
  • 45-second rounds. Swap attacker and defender after each round.
💡 Key ThingWatch the ball, not the player's feet or body — they can fake with their body but can't fake where the ball goes.
Defensive block ·
Attackers ·
Coach (holds ball) · Arrow = block shifts with ball
Block Shape
5:50–6:12 · 22 min
Defensive Block — Shift Together
All 7 defenders move as one unit when the ball moves. Like a sliding door — everyone shifts the same direction at the same time. No individual chasing.
  • Coach holds the ball and walks it slowly left, right, and forward. Defenders shift with every move.
  • Key rule: Stay within 5 yards of the person next to you. If there's a gap — "Close the door!"
  • Add 4 attackers passing the ball. Defenders shift with every pass. Don't rush at the ball — hold the line.
  • Progress to 7 attackers vs 7 defenders. Defenders hold shape until ball comes into the box.
